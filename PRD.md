**Product Requirements Document (PRD)**  
**Product Name (Working Title):** Ledgerly  
**Author:** Kobin Tim, Chief Product Officer  
**Target Markets:**

- Home users / households
- Micro-businesses & mom-and-pop stores (1–10 employees)￼**Geographies (Phase 1):** India, Southeast Asia￼**Platforms:** Mobile (Android first), Web
 
**1. Problem Statement**  
**1.1 For Home Users**

- Finances are tracked **mentally or in fragmented tools** (notes, WhatsApp, Excel).
- No real-time visibility into:
    - Monthly spending
    - Recurring bills
    - Savings vs expenses
- Financial anxiety due to lack of forecasting.

**1.2 For Mom & Pop Stores**

- Bookkeeping is **manual, error-prone**, and non-compliant.
- Pain points:
    - Cash vs digital mismatch
    - No profit clarity
    - GST/tax filing stress
    - Dependency on accountants
- Existing tools are **too complex or too expensive**.
 
**2. Product Vision**  
**Make financial clarity effortless for every household and small store — without accounting jargon or heavy setup.**  
**North Star Metric:**  
📈 _Monthly Active Users with ≥80% financial coverage (expenses + income logged)_
 
**3. Target Users & Personas**  
**3.1 Home User Persona**

- Age: 25–45
- Income: Salaried / Freelance
- Needs:
    - Expense tracking
    - Budget alerts
    - Savings insights
- Tech comfort: Medium

**3.2 Store Owner Persona**

- Age: 30–55
- Business: Kirana, salon, stationery, pharmacy
- Needs:
    - Daily sales tracking
    - Expense & supplier tracking
    - Simple reports for tax/accountant
- Tech comfort: Low–Medium
 
**4. Product Scope**  
**In Scope**

- Income & expense tracking
- Simple bookkeeping
- Automated insights
- Compliance-friendly exports

**Out of Scope (Phase 1)**

- Payroll
- Inventory management
- Credit/loan marketplace
 
**5. Key Features**
 
**5.1 Core Features (Shared)**  
**✅** **Unified Ledger**

- Cash, UPI, bank, card entries
- Manual + auto import (bank SMS parsing)
- Category tagging (AI-assisted)

**✅** **Smart Insights**

- Monthly burn rate
- Spending anomalies
- Cashflow warnings
- “You’re overspending on X” nudges

**✅** **Recurring Transactions**

- Rent, utilities, subscriptions
- Missed payment alerts
 
**5.2 Home User Features**

|   |   |
|---|---|
|**Feature**|**Description**|
|Budget Planner|Monthly category budgets|
|Goal Tracking|Save for vacation, education|
|Family View|Shared household ledger|
|Reports|Monthly PDF summary|
 
**5.3 Mom & Pop Store Features**

|   |   |
|---|---|
|**Feature**|**Description**|
|Daily Sales Entry|Simple total sales input|
|Expense Capture|Supplier payments, rent|
|Profit Snapshot|Daily / weekly profit|
|GST-Ready Reports|Accountant-friendly exports|
|Multi-Device Access|Owner + staff|
 
**6. UX Principles**

1. **Zero accounting terms**
2. **\< 3 taps** to log a transaction
3. **Local language support**
4. **Offline-first design**
5. **Trust-first visuals** (bank-grade UI)
 
**7. Non-Functional Requirements**

- Data encryption at rest & transit
- Daily automated backups
- 99.5% uptime
- Scales to 1M users
- GDPR + India DPDP compliant
 
**8. SaaS vs Shrink-Wrapped: Decision**  
**8.1 Shrink-Wrapped Product?**  
❌ **Not suitable**

- Requires frequent updates (tax rules, bank formats)
- Needs real-time insights
- High device diversity

**8.2 SaaS Product?**  
✅ **Strong Fit**  
**Reasons:**

- Continuous feature delivery
- Subscription-based affordability
- Cloud sync across devices
- AI-driven insights improve over time

📌 **Final Decision:**  
👉 **Mobile-first SaaS with optional offline mode**
 
**9. Monetization Strategy**  
**9.1 Home Users**

- Free tier (basic tracking)
- Premium: ₹199/month
    - Advanced insights
    - Family sharing
    - Export reports

**9.2 Store Owners**

- Starter: ₹299/month
- Pro: ₹599/month
    - GST reports
    - Multi-user access
    - Priority support
