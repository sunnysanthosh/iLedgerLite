**📄** **2. Investor One-Pager**  
**LedgerLite**  
**Tagline**  
The simplest financial management platform for households and small businesses.
 
**Problem**  
Millions of households and small businesses still rely on manual or fragmented financial tracking systems that limit visibility, forecasting, and compliance readiness.
 
**Solution**  
LedgerLite delivers a mobile-first SaaS platform that combines:

- Personal finance tracking
- Small business bookkeeping
- AI-powered financial insights
- Offline usability
 
**Market Opportunity**

- Massive underserved micro-business sector
- Increasing digital payment adoption
- Rising financial literacy demand
 
**Product Advantages**

- Designed for non-accountants
- AI automation reduces manual data entry
- Built for low connectivity markets
- Affordable subscription pricing
 
**Business Model**  
Freemium SaaS platform  
Revenue:

- Premium subscriptions
- Add-on compliance tools
- Embedded fintech partnerships
 
**Traction Goals**

- Rapid onboarding via mobile app ecosystem
- Community-driven small business adoption
- Regional language expansion
 
**Vision**  
Create the operating system for financial management across emerging market households and micro businesses.
