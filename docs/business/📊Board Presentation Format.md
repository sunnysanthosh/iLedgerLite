**LedgerLite – Board Product Overview**  
**Vision**  
Become the most trusted financial management platform for households and small retail businesses in emerging markets.
 
**Market Opportunity**

- Over 300M small businesses globally remain under-digitized
- Household financial management adoption is rapidly growing
- Rising digital payments create demand for transaction consolidation
 
**Core Customer Segments**

- Household finance managers
- Small retail store owners
- Micro entrepreneurs & freelancers
 
**Product Differentiation**

- Dual personal + business finance modes
- AI-driven automation
- Offline-first usability
- Local compliance integration
 
**Product Offering**  
LedgerLite provides:

- Expense and income tracking
- Sales and purchase bookkeeping
- Credit ledger management
- Financial analytics dashboards
- Tax-ready financial summaries
 
**Monetization Strategy**  
Freemium SaaS model  
Revenue Streams:

- Subscription upgrades
- Premium analytics
- Accounting integrations
- Payment ecosystem partnerships
 
**Product Roadmap**  
**Phase 1 — MVP**

- Personal tracking
- Business ledger
- Analytics dashboards

**Phase 2 — Automation**

- AI categorization
- Invoice OCR
- Banking integrations

**Phase 3 — Scale**

- Compliance automation
- Multi-user roles
- Web dashboard
 
**Strategic Success Metrics**

- MAU growth
- Subscription conversion rate
- User retention
- Ledger usage frequency
 
**Investment Ask (Example Placeholder)**

- Engineering scale

- Regional expansion GTM
