# iLedgerLite: Branding & Marketing Guide

To help you showcase `iLedgerLite` to early customers and stakeholders, I have developed a set of initial branding assets and a strategy for rollout.

## 🎨 Visual Identity

`iLedgerLite` is positioned as a **trustworthy, modern, and lightweight** financial assistant for households and small businesses.

- **Primary Colors**: 
  - **Deep Navy Blue**: Symbolizes stability, professionalism, and security.
  - **Teal / Cyan**: Represents growth, modernity, and a "lite" digital experience.
  - **Clean White**: For clarity and a minimalist, focused UI.
- **Typography**: Clean, geometric sans-serif (e.g., *Inter* or *Outfit*) to convey efficiency and readability on mobile devices.

## 🚀 How to Showcase Your Product

To build a professional brand presence, I recommend the following steps:

### 1. Build a "Coming Soon" Landing Page
Use the **Marketing Banner** on a simple landing page. 
- **Goal**: Collect early-access emails.
- **Tools**: Frameworks like Next.js (which you already use in your web dashboard) or a simple static site.

### 2. Social Media Presence (LinkedIn/X/Instagram)
Use the **Social Media Post** to announce your progress.
- **Narrative**: "Building the future of household accounting for SEA. iLedgerLite is now in staging."
- Post regular "sprint updates" based on your `ROADMAP.md` to build building-in-public credibility.

### 3. Developer Documentation & GitHub
Update your `README.md` with the **Modern Logo**. A professional header immediately increases the perceived value of an open-source or proprietary repository.

### 4. Investor / Partner Deck
If you are pitching to partners, use these high-resolution graphics to show that `iLedgerLite` is not just a collection of services, but a cohesive product and brand.
