# Walkthrough: iLedgerLite Branding & Iconography

In this project, we successfully rebooted the visual identity of `iLedgerLite` to match a modern, dynamic, and professional "iL" brand mark.

## 🚀 Accomplishments

### 1. Brand Identity & Visual Language
- **Triple-Color Palette**: Established a professional scheme of Navy Blue (`#1B2D4B`), Vibrant Orange (`#E68A2E`), and Medium Blue (`#2B6BAE`).
- **Slanted Aesthetic**: Defined a design language themed around the "iL" mark—featuring 15-20° slants to communicate speed and forward motion.

### 2. Marketing Collaterals
- **Modern Logo**: Created a high-resolution logo featuring the refined slanted 'L' typography.
- **Premium App Banner**: Designed a professional banner for web headers and social media.
- **Social Media Post**: Developed a launch-ready graphic focused on human-centric small business growth.

### 3. Unified SVG Iconography Library
- **9 Core Icons**: Created custom, pixel-perfect SVGs for:
  - **App Icon**: The official "iL" brand mark.
  - **Income, Expense, Transfer, Account, Ledger, AI, Reports, Notifications, Sync, Settings**.
- **Source-Level Consistency**: All icons are resolution-independent and use the new 3-color palette.

## 📁 Project Integration
All branding assets are now permanently housed in your project directory at:
- `assets/branding/`: Core logos and marketing PNGs.
- `assets/branding/icons/`: The full SVG library.
- `assets/branding/README.md`: Developer guide with color codes and icon specs.

## ✅ Verification
- **Visual Sync**: All assets have been manually verified to match the specific geometry of the user-provided "iL" mark.
- **Documentation**: The `branding.md` guide has been updated with full implementation details for the developer team.
