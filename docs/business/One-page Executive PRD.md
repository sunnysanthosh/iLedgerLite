**📄** **Executive Product Requirements Document (PRD)**  
**Product Name**  
**LedgerLite** _(Working Title)_  
**Product Type**  
**Mobile-first SaaS Finance Management Platform** with offline capability
 
**🎯** **Product Vision**  
Empower households and small local businesses to effortlessly manage, understand, and grow their finances using a simple, intelligent, and affordable digital platform.
 
**🧩** **Problem Statement**  
Home users and small retail businesses commonly rely on manual bookkeeping or complex accounting tools that:

- Provide poor financial visibility
- Require accounting knowledge
- Fail to automate transactions and insights
- Create stress around tax and compliance
- Do not unify cash and digital payments
 
**💡** **Product Solution**  
LedgerLite delivers a **dual-mode finance platform** designed for:  
**🏠** **Home Users**

- Expense & income tracking
- Budget planning
- Bill reminders
- Savings goal monitoring
- AI-driven spending insights

**🏪** **Mom & Pop Stores**

- Sales and purchase tracking
- Customer credit ledger
- Supplier payment management
- Profit & cash flow dashboards
- Tax-ready summaries
 
**⭐** **Core Value Proposition**

- Ultra-simple, non-accountant-friendly interface
- AI automation for financial insights
- Offline-first mobile usability
- Local compliance & tax readiness
- Affordable subscription model
 
**👥** **Target Users**  
**Primary**

- Household financial planners
- Small retail store owners
- Micro entrepreneurs

**Secondary**

- Freelancers
- Home-based businesses
