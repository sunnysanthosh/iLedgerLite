**🧭** **4. Product Strategy Memo**  
**Strategic Objective**  
Develop a scalable financial platform that simplifies money management for underserved household and small business users.
 
**Strategic Pillars**  
**1. Extreme Simplicity**  
Remove accounting complexity through automation and intuitive UI.  
**2. AI-Driven Intelligence**  
Automate categorization, forecasting, and insights.  
**3. Accessibility**  
Provide offline functionality and regional language support.  
**4. Financial Ecosystem Integration**  
Enable payments, compliance, lending, and accounting integrations.
 
**Strategic Priorities**  
**Short Term**

- MVP development
- Core ledger infrastructure
- Customer validation

**Mid Term**

- AI automation
- Banking integrations
- Compliance modules

**Long Term**

- Embedded finance services
- Credit scoring platform
- Supplier marketplace
 
**Strategic Risks**

- User trust in financial apps
- Data security expectations
- Competition from accounting software

Mitigation includes strong encryption, free tier adoption, and simplicity-first design.
